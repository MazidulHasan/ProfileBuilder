About Me

Senior QA Automation Engineer with 6+ years of experience in software testing, automation, and CI/CD. Remote-ready professional based in Dhaka, Bangladesh.

My Journey

With over 6 years of experience as a Senior QA Automation Engineer, I've specialized in designing and implementing robust automation frameworks using Selenium, Cypress, Playwright, and Rest Assured. At Therap (BD) Ltd, I've led regression, feature, integration, and UAT testing while boosting API test efficiency by 75% and enhancing UI test coverage by 25%. My expertise spans across healthcare and finance domains, delivering quality solutions for individuals with intellectual and developmental disabilities.

My Approach

I bring a shift-left mindset to quality assurance, conducting in-depth requirement analysis to align testing strategies with project architecture and business goals. Proficient in Agile/Scrum methodologies, I excel at CI/CD integration using Jenkins, Docker, and Git. Whether building load-testing frameworks with Gatling, managing defect tracking in JIRA, or collaborating across distributed multicultural teams, my goal is to ensure high-quality releases through comprehensive testing coverage and early defect detection.
